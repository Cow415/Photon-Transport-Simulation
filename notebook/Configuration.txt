Parameter	    Physical Origin

To calculate the parameters, the imaginary refractive index is required.

Tomocube's holotomography (HT) technology and TomoStudio X software are designed to process complex 3D Refractive Index (RI) 
tomograms, enabling label-free, quantitative, and 3D visualization of live cells and tissues.

The real refractive index measures how much a material slows down and bends light (phase velocity). 
The complex refractive index combines this with an imaginary component (the extinction coefficient), which quantifies 
how much light is absorbed or attenuated by the material.
