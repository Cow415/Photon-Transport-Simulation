Pipeline:
RI data unpack (Fiji) --> data/raw/
      |
      V
Geometry Extraction
   Filter & Obtain RI of cell geometry as a matrix
      |   cell_ri_extract.ipynb
      |   para_ri_extract.ipynb      
      V  
   data/processed --> data/extracted ri matrix
   ↓
Preparing Body
   Adjust body position if needed & pad gel/air RI into the Filter, crop to square frame
      |  
      V  
Photon Monte Carlo simulation(By pmcxcl)|| Flux Mapping as a 3D Matrix with defined time variation

Full Steps:
   Extraction: 
      Low-pass Fourier
      Median
      Guassian Blur
      Null Neighbor Trimming
      Multi-Otsu Region identifying
      Apply Region Mask to Original Matrix to Extract Cell RI

   Preparation:
      Crop desired frame as a square
      Pad gel region below cell that is null space, assign all else as air
      Assign label to parasitic region of the cell
      pad into "Swiss-Roll" by 100 pixel layers

   Configuration:
      1000000 photons 
      set unit to 1/1000 of milimeter -> Micron

      10 picosecond frames
      each frame 1 picoseconds apart

      Light source positioned on gel by the edge of the frame
      Angled 45 degree to horizontal, aimed into the gel medium
      Beam configuration of light source

      Media Labels:
         Air = 1
         Gel = 2
         Cell = 3
         Parasitic Region = 4
